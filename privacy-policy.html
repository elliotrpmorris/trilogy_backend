Privacy Policy for Trilogy Health Application

Introduction


Trilogy Health Limited (company number: 14559481) ("Trilogy Health", "we", "our", "us") operates this mobile app ("Mobile App"). Hence, Trilogy Health processes the personal data of its customers and potential customers (herein after jointly referred to as "customers") and adheres to the applicable regulations pertaining to personal data protection.
This Privacy Policy describes how Trilogy Health collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our exercise, nutrition, and physiotherapy mobile application (the "Trilogy Health App"). By using the App, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. Please read the following carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

Information we collect


1. Personal Information: When you use our App, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, age, sex, height, weight, and fitness goals. We may also collect information you provide when you contact us for support or feedback.
2. Health and Fitness Data: Our App will collect information about your health and fitness activities, including exercise routines, workouts, nutritional requirements, medical history, and medical considerations. As part of calculating your nutritional requirements, we require your sex, height, and weight. This information may be collected automatically through the App or entered manually by you. The health and fitness information is not advice and should not be treated as such.
3. Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use the App, such as the features you access, the time and duration of your use, and interactions with the App.
4. Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the App, including the device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
This Mobile App provides only information, is not medical or treatment advice and may not be treated as such by the individual. As such, this App may not be relied upon for the purposes of medical diagnosis or as a recommendation for medical care or treatment. The information on this App is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis or treatment.
Under General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) we will ensure that your personal data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently, without adversely affecting your rights. We will only process your personal data if at least one of the following basis applies:
• You have provided consent to the processing of your personal data for one or more specific purposes;
• Processing is necessary for the performance of a contract to which you are a party or in order to take steps at the request of you prior to entering into a contract;
• Processing is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation to which we are subject;
• Processing is necessary to protect the vital interests of you or of another natural person;
• Processing is necessary for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest or in the exercise of official authority vested in the controller; and/or
• Processing is necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by us or by a third party, except where such interests are overridden by the fundamental rights and freedoms of the data subject which require protection of personal data, in particular where the data subject is a child.
If you are aged under 13 you must not use the App as it is not designed for you. We do not intend to collect the information of anyone under 13. If you are aware that any information of anyone under 13 has been shared with the App, please let us know so that we can delete that data.
If you are aged under 18, we recommend that you speak to an adult that you trust if you have any difficulties reaching an informed decision regarding the activation of any use of your information or our treatment of your information.

How we use what we collect


1. Provide and Improve the App: We use the information we collect to provide and improve our App, including personalising your experience, analysing usage trends, and developing new features and functionalities.
2. Health and Fitness Management: We may use your information to track your progress, provide insights, recommendations, and personalised content related to exercise, nutrition, and physiotherapy to help you achieve your health and fitness goals.
3. Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about your account, provide customer support, send you updates and announcements, and respond to your inquiries and feedback.
4. Legal Compliance: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforce our rights arising from any contracts.

How we share your information


1. Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as data analytics, hosting, customer support, and marketing.
2. Health Professionals: With your explicit consent, we may share your health and fitness data with licensed healthcare professionals, such as nutritionists and physiotherapists, to provide you with personalized recommendations and treatment plans.
3. Aggregated and De-identified Data: We may aggregate and de-identify your information to create anonymized statistics and insights for research and analytical purposes.
4. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforce our rights arising from any contracts.
